WHAT IS AKANA NAME?


The Akan people of Ghana and the Ivory Coast frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. This naming tradition is shared throughout West Africa and the African diaspora.
